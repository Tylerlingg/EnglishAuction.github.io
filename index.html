How to Use pepeDex

  1. Connect your MetaMask wallet by clicking the "Connect to MetaMask" button.
  2. Once connected, you can interact with the pepeDex smart contract.
  3. Add liquidity by entering the amount of tokens you want to add and click "Add Liquidity".
  4. Swap tokens for ETH by entering the amount and click "Swap".
  5. Claim accumulated fees by clicking "Claim Fees".

pepeDex is an automated market maker (AMM) that allows users to swap tokens, add liquidity, and remove liquidity. Liquidity providers can also claim their share of accumulated fees. It uses the Pepe token and integrates with Uniswap V3.
